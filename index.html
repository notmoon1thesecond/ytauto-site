YTAUTO

A personal short-form video tool for a single YouTube channel.

YTAUTO edits short movie and TV scene clips — sourced from official studio/network trailer and promo uploads — into vertical Shorts with captions and royalty-free music, then uploads them to one creator's own YouTube channel via the YouTube Data API.

It is a single-user tool: it uploads only to the channel of the person who authorizes it, and does not access, request, or process data belonging to any other user or account.

See the Privacy Policy for details on what data this app accesses and how it's used.
